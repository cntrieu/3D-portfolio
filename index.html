My Work 02

TinySquares

TinySquares is a website where users can journal daily using pixels. Users can create multiple journals for different reasons and create their own legend with a name and colour to fill in their journals.

Technologies: MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js

Nomad Nebula

Nomad Nebula is a social media platform for people who love space and stargazing! Users can create an account, post to a greater community with many other users where users can post, like, comment, and share posts in the community.

Technologies: Sequelize, MySQL, Express.js, Express Handlebars, Cloudinary, Heroku
